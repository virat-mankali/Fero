About us

Here is everything you should know about us.

Project Fero’ is a groundbreaking initiative aimed at revolutionizing the current public transportation booking and travelling system. Initially founded by C. Rahul Reddy, Virat Mankali and Co-founded by K. Bhavesh Reddy, and Jashwanth Vasadi, our mission is to provide a smart, user-friendly, and multipurpose public transportation system that caters to the needs of the common people to fulfill their urge in a digitalized way.


The name "Fero" translates to "trip" in Icelandic, reflecting our commitment to enhancing every journey. Our platform integrates various public transport options, including buses, trains, and metro services, into a seamless booking and tracking experience. By utilizing our app or website, users can easily navigate through a comprehensive system that simplifies travel planning, reduces travel time, and enhances overall convenience.


Our Vision

In a densely populated country like India, managing traffic and pollution is a significant challenge. Project Fero' addresses these issues by promoting increased use of public transport, thereby reducing traffic congestion and pollution. Our system offers real-time tracking and booking features, allowing users to find and book the best transport options available around them with just a few clicks.


Key Features

  1. Self-Tracking and Pictorial Navigation: Navigate easily and track your journey with our intuitive pictorial guides.
  2. Comprehensive Public Transport Booking: Book buses, trains, and metro services all in one place.
  3. Secure Payment Methods: Make secure and convenient payments directly through the app.
  4. Safety and Security: In case of emergencies, users can send an immediate "HELP ME" message to our control room, ensuring rapid response from the nearest assistance team, including police and medical personnel.
  5. Direct Access Information: Seamless communication between users, admin, and vehicle in-charge for smooth travel experiences.


Our Objectives

  • To provide a smart, user-friendly public transportation tracking and booking system.
  • To enhance the reliability and efficiency of public transport.
  • To ensure safety and security for all users.
  • To offer a multipurpose platform that eliminates inconvenience.
