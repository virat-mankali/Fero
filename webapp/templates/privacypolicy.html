PRIVACY POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

  • Purpose: This Privacy Policy outlines how Project Fero' (referred to as "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and mobile application.
  • Scope: Applies to all users of the Project Fero' platform, including users who access, browse, or register on our website and app.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Personal Information:
    • Name, contact details (email, phone number) and address.
    • Payment details (credit/debit card information) are needed for processing transactions.
    • Profile information (social media login data if used for sign-up).
  • Non-Personal Information:
    • Device information (IP address, device type, operating system).
    • Usage data (pages visited, time spent on the platform).
    • Location data to offer nearby transportation options.


3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Service Delivery: To provide and improve our services, including booking and tracking public transport, and ensuring smooth transactions.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, suggest transportation options, and remember your preferences.
  • Communication: To send you notifications, alerts, and updates regarding your bookings or service changes.
  • Security: To monitor and enhance the security of our platform, including using location data for safety features like emergency assistance.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests.

4. INFORMATION SHARING

  • With Service Providers: Sharing information with third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, customer support) who assist in delivering our services.
  • With Public Authorities: Sharing information with law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law or for safety and security purposes.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

5. DATA SECURITY

  • Protection Measures: We implement security measures such as encryption and secure servers to protect your data.
  • User Responsibilities: Users are responsible for safeguarding their login credentials and should notify us immediately if they suspect any unauthorized access to their account.

6. USER RIGHTS

  • Access and Correction: Users can access and update their personal information through their account settings.
  • Data Portability: Users can request a copy of their data in a structured format.
  • Deletion: Users can request the deletion of their account and personal data, subject to certain legal or operational requirements.

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

  • Usage: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, track usage patterns, and deliver personalized content.
  • User Control: Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

8. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

  • External Sites: Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.
  • Social media: Integrations with social media platforms (e.g. Google, X) are provided for user convenience, but any data shared with these platforms is governed by their privacy policies.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

  • Age Restriction: Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
  • Parental Controls: If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, they should contact us immediately.

10. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

  • Updates: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. Users will be notified of significant changes.
  • Acceptance: Continued use of our services after changes signifies acceptance of the revised policy.
